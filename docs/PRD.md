# PRD: BIGBoss Trading Organization OS

## Founder Positioning

From struggle to mastery, the founder turned seven years of trading lessons into books, indicators, and MT5 bots. The brand shares tools, skills, and mindset to help traders win and keep winning.

## Strategic Focus

The strongest opportunity is **AI + Trading Education Infrastructure**.

The business should focus on:

- AI Trading College.
- BIGBoss Trader OS.
- Market structure education.
- Trading psychology.
- Liquidity and institutional behavior.
- AI trade review.
- MT5 bot research and validation.
- Scalable digital products.
- Faceless content and recurring subscription revenue.

## Core Theme

"The Science of Why Markets Move."

The product space includes:

- How money is created.
- How central banks move markets.
- Global financial system education.
- Forex market structure and liquidity.
- Smart money versus retail money.
- Trading psychology and cognitive biases.
- How AI reads financial markets.
- How hedge funds think.
- The life cycle of a trade.
- The institutional trader's playbook.

## V1 Product Direction

Build a Trading Organization OS with a central AI brain and specialist agents. The first version should help the founder command, coordinate, and advance:

- AI Trading College.
- BIGBoss Trader OS.
- Content creation.
- Trade review.
- MT5 bot research.
- Education products.
- Product launches.

## Out Of Scope For V1

- Live trade execution.
- Placing orders.
- Fully autonomous financial decisions.
- Full architecture/construction AI system.
- Payment, billing, or CRM automation unless added later.
